About

Born and raised in one of the largest Puerto Rican communities in America, WRDS has always been surrounded by music and culture. He was drawn to the rhythm and poetry of hip-hop, and began writing lyrics as a way to express himself. Over the years, he has developed a unique style that is both ahead of its time and deeply rooted in tradition.

As a prolific songwriter, he is always pushing himself to create innovative music, blending classic hip-hop with modern production techniques. A Chicago emcee through and through, he never quits and always raises the bar for himself, constantly striving to improve and evolve as an artist
